Butter Roti

  • to taste salt
  • 50 gms butter

Recipe

  • Step 1: Make a soft dough with all ingredience except butter and maida
  • Step 2:Keep 30 min
  • Step 3: Make a medium boll and spread it like chappathy
  • Step 4:In a tawa grill it and top with butterw
